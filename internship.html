Dual Internships Experience

Yuvaraj Anbarasu â€¢ July 28

During this eventful summer, I embarked on an exciting journey by participating in not just one but two internships concurrently. While the experience was undeniably hectic, juggling my regular job and the responsibilities of two internships on consecutive days taught me invaluable time management and multitasking skills.

To start off my internships focused on two distinct areas of Computer Science. My main internship at Georgia State University focused on Data Analytics and followed a project-based learning schedule. My secondary internship was with American Megatrends and focused on learning about firmware and hardware using in AMI through the use of demos and projects.

From the onset, I knew this summer would be demanding. However, I embraced the challenge with enthusiasm and determination. Each day brought new opportunities for learning and growth as I navigated through different professional environments.

One of the main takeaways from these internship experiences is my immense sense of accomplishment throughout the process. Moreover, being a part of two distinct organizations allowed me to gain diverse insights into various industries in the Computer Science field and work cultures. Each internship offered a unique perspective and exposed me to different aspects of the professional world, broadening my understanding of potential career paths and helping me refine my future aspirations.

The support and guidance provided by my mentors allowed me to develop personally and professionally. I was encouraged to ask questions, voice my ideas, and actively contribute to projects, making me feel like a valued member of each team.

Notably, my ability to adapt and quickly transition from one internship to the next proved to be a significant asset. The experience of handling diverse responsibilities and meeting distinct expectations enabled me to become more flexible, resilient, and adaptable as a professional. This internship experience gave me a newfound goal of learning about every aspect of the Computer Science field.

In conclusion, this summer interning with two organizations was a whirlwind of experiences, challenges, and accomplishments. I am immensely grateful for the opportunity to have been a part of these internships and to grow personally and professionally. I will carry the lessons learned and the connections made forward, as they will undoubtedly play a crucial role in shaping my future endeavors.
